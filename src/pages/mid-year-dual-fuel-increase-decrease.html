How does your monthly Direct Debit work?

You are paying by Direct Debit with a fixed amount every month. We check your Direct Debit amount against your meter readings halfway through your plan and again at the end to make sure you’re paying the right amount.

We do this to try and make sure you don’t end up in credit or debit on your account at the end of your plan.
