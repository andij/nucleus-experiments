Compensation Arrangements

British Gas Services Limited and British Gas Insurance Limited are covered by the Financial Services Compensation Scheme (FSCS). You may be entitled to compensation from the scheme if we cannot meet our obligations. General insurance is covered for 90% of the claim, without any upper limit. Further information about the compensation scheme is available from FSCS at www.fscs.org.uk or telephone 0800 678 1100 or 020 7741 4100.
