Your Installation. Before you start sending photos

We need you to provide us with some information and images of your property.

Your property and photos for installation

This is so we can see if your home is suitable for the installation.

The photo's you'll need

  • A photo of the front of your property and any off-street parking.
  • A close-up photo of your fuse box/consumer unit.
  • A photo of the surrounding area of your fuse box/consumer unit.
  • Close-up photo of your electricity meter.
  • A photo of the surrounding area of your main gas meter.
  • A close-up photo of your water meter.
  • A photo of the surrounding area of your main water meter.
